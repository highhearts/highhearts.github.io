Hi.

Music can soothe the mind, and provide some small comfort in an often cruel world. It can make the hair stand up on the backs of our necks. It can make us feel alive.

Music brings people together, and we are all in this together. We must be. Music is responsible for our most treasured friendships, keeping us close.

Music is a language. It can express ideas that other languages cannot. Deftly controlled vibrations of air can reach through the calcified armor of even the most hardened heart.

In this way, music can be used as a tool; a weapon even. It can be a culture bomb that can start a revolution, and the world needs a revolution.

This is our manifesto; this is why we make music.

- High Hearts
